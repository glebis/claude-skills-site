Health Data queries your Apple Health SQLite database directly — vitals, activity, sleep, workouts — in Markdown, JSON, or FHIR format. Wispr Analytics mines your voice dictation history for work patterns, sentiment trends, and productivity insights. Chrome History reveals what you've been reading and when. DoctorG bridges personal health data with medical research. Temple Generator creates a 3D interactive Three.js visualization of your entire Obsidian vault — a spatial map of everything you know.
