Deep Research uses OpenAI's research API for comprehensive internet sweeps on any topic. Firecrawl Research scrapes specific sites systematically, generates BibTeX bibliographies, and supports academic paper writing with Pandoc templates. DoctorG answers health questions using tiered trusted sources with GRADE-style evidence ratings and integrates your Apple Health data for personalized context. Chrome and browsing history skills let you search what you've already read by date, keywords, or content type.
