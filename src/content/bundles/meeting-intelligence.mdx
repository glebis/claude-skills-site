Connect to Fathom or Granola to pull transcripts automatically after every call. The transcript analyzer extracts decisions, action items, and terminology. The meeting processor classifies each meeting by type — sales call, coaching session, internal sync — and runs type-specific analysis. YouTube transcript pulls talks and lectures into the same format. ElevenLabs converts any summary to audio for listening on the go.
