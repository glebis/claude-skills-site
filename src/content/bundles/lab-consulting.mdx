Agency Docs Updater is a full pipeline: it takes a lab meeting recording, transcribes it, generates metadata, uploads to YouTube, and publishes the meeting notes to a docs site — one command. Lab Retro runs structured end-of-cohort retrospectives with progress audits, best-prompt reviews, and forward planning. Context Builder generates interactive discovery session prompts for consulting clients, guiding them through business context, pain points, and AI opportunities. Recording mode redacts PII in real time during screen-shares and demos.
