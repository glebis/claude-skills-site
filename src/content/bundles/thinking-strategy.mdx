Balanced mode forces multi-perspective analysis and constructive disagreement — it pushes back instead of agreeing. Decision Toolkit generates interactive step-by-step guides, bias checklists, and scenario explorers for hard choices. JTBD runs Jobs-to-Be-Done interviews, mines customer reviews for switching forces, and scores opportunities with structured output. Skill Studio interviews you about any workflow you want to automate and produces a one-page spec with an SVG design map.
