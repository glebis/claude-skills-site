## What it does

Provides terminal-based access to Google NotebookLM for creating and managing notebooks, adding sources, and generating audio overviews. Bridges the gap between local research workflows and NotebookLM's synthesis capabilities without leaving the command line.

## Key features

- **Notebook management** — create, list, and organize NotebookLM notebooks from terminal
- **Source ingestion** — add documents, URLs, and text to notebooks programmatically
- **Audio overviews** — trigger NotebookLM's podcast-style audio generation
- **Research integration** — feed vault notes and research documents directly into NotebookLM

## When to use

When you want to leverage NotebookLM's AI synthesis and audio overview features as part of a terminal-based research workflow, without switching to the browser.
