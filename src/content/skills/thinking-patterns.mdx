## What it does

Processes months of Fathom meeting transcripts through a multi-stage parallel agent pipeline: extracts your speech, analyzes 12 cognitive dimensions per transcript (Burns' distortions, hedging, metaphors, decision heuristics, etc.), then synthesizes cross-session patterns including contradictions, blind spots, and execution gaps between stated priorities and actual focus.

## Key features

- **12 extraction dimensions** — cognitive distortions, problem framing, conceptual metaphors, hedging/certainty, code-switching, decision moments, emotional indicators, avoidance, agency language, competing commitments, role markers, energy signals
- **10 output sections** — recurring narratives, problem framing, metaphors, decision heuristics, avoided topics, contradictions, energy patterns, role shifts, execution gap, biases
- **Multi-agent architecture** — 10-13 parallel Sonnet agents for extraction, 5 for synthesis
- **Evidence-based** — every finding includes dated quotes from transcripts
- **Blind spot detection** — "The 5 Things You Don't See" summary

## When to use

Quarterly or after accumulating 10+ transcripts, when you want an honest external perspective on your cognitive patterns, decision-making habits, and the gap between what you say you prioritize and what you actually do. Costs approximately $3.50 per run.
