## What it does

Converts markdown to professional-looking PDFs using Pandoc with the Eisvogel template. Produces publication-quality documents with proper typography, table of contents, and color-coded title pages.

## Key features

- **Two layouts** — desktop/print (A4 with 2.5cm margins) and mobile-friendly (6x9 for phone reading)
- **Bilingual** — full support for English and Russian documents, with EB Garamond for beautiful Cyrillic
- **Theme colors** — blue for white papers, green for marketing, purple for research, gray for technical docs
- **Title pages** — customizable colored title pages with subtitle and author via YAML frontmatter
- **TOC** — auto-generated table of contents with configurable depth

## When to use

When you need a polished PDF from markdown — white papers, research documents, proposals, marketing materials, or technical documentation in English or Russian.
