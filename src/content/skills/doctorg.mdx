## What it does

Answers health and wellness questions using only trusted, evidence-based sources with explicit evidence strength ratings. Searches tiered sources — from PubMed and Cochrane (Tier 1) down to quality journalism (Tier 4) — and presents findings with GRADE-inspired confidence levels.

## Depth levels

- **Quick** (default, ~30s) — WebSearch across Tier 1–2 sources for simple factual questions
- **Deep** (~90s) — adds Tavily search across Tier 1–3 for nuanced topics with competing claims
- **Full** (~3 min) — adds Firecrawl for primary source scraping on controversial topics

## Key features

- **Tiered evidence** — PubMed/Cochrane > Mayo Clinic/Cleveland Clinic > Examine.com/STAT News > quality journalism
- **Category-aware** — auto-detects nutrition, exercise, sleep, medication, or mental health topics and prioritizes relevant sources
- **Personal context** — integrates Apple Health data when available for personalized answers

## When to use

Any health, nutrition, exercise, sleep, or wellness question where you want evidence-based answers rather than generic advice.
