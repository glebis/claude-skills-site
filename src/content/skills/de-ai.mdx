## What it does

Identifies and removes AI tells at six levels (structural, sentence, lexical, voice, rhetorical, predictability) and rewrites text to sound authentically human. Unlike commercial de-AI tools that focus on detection evasion, this prioritizes meaning preservation, interactive context gathering, and language-specific optimization.

## Key features

- **6-level diagnosis** — structural uniformity, sentence patterns, stock vocabulary, emotional flatness, meta-signposting, predictability
- **5 languages** — English, Russian, German, Spanish, French with language-specific tell patterns
- **6 register types** — personal, essay, critique, narrative, technical, academic
- **Interactive mode** — asks about purpose, audience, and constraints before processing
- **Explain mode** — shows exactly which AI tells were removed and why
- **No word limits** — processes full documents unlike commercial tools (125-300 word caps)

## When to use

When you have AI-assisted draft text that needs to sound genuinely human — blog posts, business emails, technical docs, or multilingual marketing content.
