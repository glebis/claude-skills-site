## What it does

Connects to the Fathom API to fetch meeting recordings, full transcripts, AI-generated summaries, action items, and participant info. Saves everything as structured markdown to your Obsidian vault.

## Key features

- **List and fetch** — browse recent meetings, fetch by ID, or pull all meetings from a specific date
- **Full transcripts** — downloads complete meeting transcripts with speaker identification
- **AI summaries** — includes Fathom's AI-generated summaries and action items
- **Video download** — optionally download the video recording (requires ffmpeg)
- **Auto-analyze** — can pipe fetched transcripts directly to the transcript-analyzer skill

## When to use

After any recorded meeting, to sync transcripts and summaries into your vault. Pairs well with meeting-processor for type-specific structured analysis.
