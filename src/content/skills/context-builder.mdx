## What it does

Generates structured discovery prompts for consulting engagements. Through an interactive intake process, it gathers context about the client company, runs automated web research, and produces a reusable multi-section prompt that guides teams through AI transformation discovery.

## Workflow

1. **Intake** — interactive questions about the company, consulting focus, industry, and existing context
2. **Research** — automated web search about the company plus vault search for existing notes and transcripts
3. **Section selection** — curated sections based on focus area (AI automation, existential strategy, new business models, product development)
4. **Output** — a resumable, multi-section discovery prompt with Express and Deep Dive modes, compiled into a reusable CLAUDE.md

## When to use

At the start of a new consulting engagement, when you need to prepare a structured discovery session that will guide the client through context gathering about their business, pain points, tech stack, and AI opportunities.
