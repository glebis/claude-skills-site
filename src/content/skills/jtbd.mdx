## What it does

A structured Jobs-to-Be-Done interview engine that helps you articulate what you're building, for whom, and why — then outputs a decision-grade artifact bundle. Captures the four switching forces (Push, Pull, Habit, Anxiety) that determine whether people will actually adopt your product.

## Modes

- **Interview** (default) — live JTBD conversation producing a full artifact bundle
- **Transcript ingest** — feed it an existing interview transcript for analysis
- **Review mining** — extract JTBD insights from customer reviews (CSV/JSON)
- **Update** — refine an existing JTBD brief with new data

## Output artifacts

- `jtbd.json` — machine-readable structured data
- `one-pager.md` — shareable summary of the job, forces, and opportunity
- `messaging-angles.md` — copy angles derived from the Switch forces
- `gtm-brief.md` — go-to-market recommendations

## When to use

When you need to articulate what a project does and why it matters, figure out what to build next, convert customer interviews or reviews into actionable insights, or generate messaging for a product.
