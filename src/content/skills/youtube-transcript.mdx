## What it does

Extracts transcripts and metadata from YouTube videos and saves them as structured markdown notes in your Obsidian vault. Works with subtitles and closed captions — does not download video or audio files.

## What you get

- **Full transcript** — complete text with timestamps from the video's subtitles
- **Metadata** — title, channel, upload date, duration, description, tags
- **Markdown note** — properly formatted with YAML frontmatter, ready for your vault

## When to use

When you want to capture the content of a YouTube video as searchable text in your knowledge base — lectures, tutorials, interviews, conference talks. Pairs well with transcript-analyzer for extracting structured insights from the transcript.
