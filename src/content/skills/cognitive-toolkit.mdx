## What it does

Runs interactive CBT and DBT guided exercises from the terminal. Starts with a mood check-in, recommends a technique, walks through the full protocol, and saves the session to your Obsidian vault. Supports configurable therapeutic pushback (gentle to firm) and optional HRV/sleep biofeedback integration.

## Key features

- **7 techniques** — thought records, opposite action, DEAR MAN, TIPP crisis skills, chain analysis, behavioral activation, wise mind
- **Configurable pushback** — Socratic questioning at gentle/moderate/firm levels, adjustable mid-session
- **Health integration** — optional HRV and sleep data as contextual framing
- **Vault output** — sessions saved with frontmatter (date, technique, mood before/after)
- **Telegram entry points** — trigger exercises from mobile via bot commands

## When to use

Between therapy sessions when you want structured practice with cognitive-behavioral techniques, or during moments of spiraling, distress, or decision paralysis.
