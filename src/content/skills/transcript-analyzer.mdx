## What it does

Runs meeting transcripts through Cerebras AI (llama-3.3-70b) to extract structured data — decisions, action items, opinions, questions, and terminology — at high speed.

## What it extracts

- **Decisions** — what was decided, by whom, with any caveats
- **Action items** — who needs to do what, with deadlines if mentioned
- **Opinions** — positions expressed by participants on key topics
- **Questions** — unresolved questions raised during the meeting
- **Terminology** — domain-specific terms and jargon used, with definitions inferred from context

## Key features

- **Fast** — Cerebras inference provides near-instant extraction even for long transcripts
- **Structured output** — clean JSON and markdown output for downstream processing
- **Glossary building** — automatically builds a glossary from specialized terms used in discussion

## When to use

When you have a meeting transcript and need to quickly extract what was decided, what needs to be done, and what was discussed — without reading the entire thing. Works well as a downstream step after Fathom or Granola sync.
