## What it does

A unified CLI for the entire Google Workspace — Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Sheets, Docs, Slides, Tasks, People, Chat, Meet, Forms, Keep, and Admin. Also includes cross-service helper workflows that combine multiple services in one command.

## Services

- **Gmail** — triage unread, search, send, archive, label, filter, drafts
- **Calendar** — today's agenda, create events, weekly view, Meet conferencing
- **Drive** — upload, list, share, download files
- **Sheets** — read ranges, append rows
- **Tasks** — create and manage tasks
- **Chat** — post messages to spaces
- **People** — search contacts

## Helper workflows

- **standup-report** — today's meetings + open tasks in one view
- **meeting-prep** — details for your next meeting
- **email-to-task** — convert an email into a task
- **weekly-digest** — week's meetings + unread summary

## When to use

Any time you'd normally open Gmail, Google Calendar, Drive, or any other Google Workspace app in a browser. The `gws` CLI handles it all from the terminal.
