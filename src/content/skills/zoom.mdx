## What it does

Interfaces with the Zoom API to create meetings, manage schedules, and access cloud recordings — all from the terminal.

## Key features

- **Create meetings** — schedule with topic, time, duration, and settings
- **List meetings** — browse upcoming and past meetings
- **Cloud recordings** — access and download Zoom cloud recordings
- **Meeting management** — update or delete scheduled meetings

## When to use

When you need to schedule a Zoom meeting, check your upcoming meetings, or download a recording without opening the Zoom app. Used by the agency-docs-updater skill to automatically download lab meeting recordings.
