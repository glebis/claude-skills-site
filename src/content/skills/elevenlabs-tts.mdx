## What it does

Converts any text into high-quality spoken audio using ElevenLabs' text-to-speech API. Supports seven pre-configured voice presets with customizable parameters for stability and expressiveness.

## Voice presets

- **rachel** (default) — clear, professional female
- **adam** — deep, authoritative male
- **bella** — warm, friendly female
- **josh** — friendly, conversational male
- **arnold** — deep, powerful male
- **ava** — expressive, dynamic female
- **elli** — young, enthusiastic female

## Key features

- **Tunable parameters** — adjust stability (consistency vs. expressiveness) and similarity boost (adherence to voice characteristics)
- **Any length** — handles short phrases to full articles
- **Auto-play** — generated audio can be played immediately via `afplay`

## When to use

When you want to hear text read aloud — article narration, content preview, accessibility, or producing audio assets for podcasts and videos.
